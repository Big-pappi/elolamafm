About El olama fm 100.3 MHz

Our Mission

El olama fm 100.3 MHz is dedicated to being the voice of Njombe, Tanzania, providing our listeners with diverse programs that entertain, inform, and connect the community.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, El olama fm has been the region's go-to radio station for news, music, and events. Over the years, we have grown to become an integral part of the Njombe community, with a mission to support local artists, organizations, and businesses.
